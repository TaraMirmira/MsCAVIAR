This data was prepared using publicly available type 2 diabetes summary statistics data from Biobank Japan (BBJ) and the UK Biobank (UKB). We also use 1000 Genomes data and a utility script from the PAINTOR software package. We would like to thank the people who made these datasets available and the authors of the corresponding papers.

Biobank Japan data website:
http://jenger.riken.jp/en/result

UK Biobank data website:
http://www.nealelab.is/uk-biobank
